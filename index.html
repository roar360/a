Ingredient Safety Guide

  • Non-Halal: Pork gelatin, alcohol, non-halal enzymes → Haram
  • WHO unsafe: Artificial trans fats, excessive nitrites, certain preservatives (BHA/BHT)
  • Country Bans: Potassium bromate (EU), Rhodamine B (India), Tartrazine (Norway)
  • For Diabetics: High fructose corn syrup, aspartame (caution), dextrose
  • High BP: Sodium chloride, MSG, sodium nitrate
  • Asthma: Sulfites, MSG, acrylamide, tartrazine
